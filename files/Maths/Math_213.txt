Lesson 11-4: Multiplying Polynomials
In order to understand how to multiply two polynomials together, it
is best to start with how to multiply a monomial and a polynomial. The
key to multiplying a monomial and a polynomial together is to use the
distributive property and the rules for multiplying numbers with the
same base.
